Sink Configuration Rules


The sink cabinet and gas stove cabinet must each be made once as default, unless otherwise specified.


The remaining space should be evenly divided.


The sink cabinet should be placed where the water pipe is located.


The gas stove cabinet should be placed near the vent.



For Built-in Wardrobes


Each unit (section) is based on a width of 750–1050 mm.


Half-units (0.5 sections) are allowed.


The total width should be evenly divided among units.


Each section includes one internal shelf by default.
